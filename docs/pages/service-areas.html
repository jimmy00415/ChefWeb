Understanding Travel Fees

Travel fees help cover our chefs' transportation costs to bring the hibachi experience to your door. The fee structure varies by state:

  • Included States (CA, NY): No additional travel fee - it's built into our pricing
  • Estimated Fee States: Travel fee ranges from $50-$125 depending on distance from our chef's base
  • Exact Fee Confirmation: We'll confirm your specific travel fee within 24 hours of your booking request
  • Transparent Pricing: Travel fees are always disclosed upfront before you finalize your booking

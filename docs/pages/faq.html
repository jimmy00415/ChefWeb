üè† Setup & Host Responsibilities

Our chefs arrive with everything needed for cooking:

  • Professional hibachi grill/griddle equipment
  • All cooking utensils and tools
  • Fresh ingredients for your selected menu
  • Cooking oils, seasonings, and sauces
  • Disposable gloves and sanitation supplies

Please provide:

  • Table space: 6-8 feet of sturdy table for the grill (folding tables work great!)
  • Electrical outlet: Standard 110V outlet within 10 feet
  • Plates, utensils, napkins: For your guests (disposable is fine)
  • Beverages: We don't provide drinks (but can add sake/beer for extra charge)
  • Trash bags: For waste disposal after cleanup

Optional: Seating for guests, tablecloth, decorations

Minimum requirements:

  • Grill area: 6-8 feet of table space
  • Chef working zone: 3-4 feet clearance around the grill
  • Guest seating: Enough room for your party to sit/stand comfortably
  • Total space: Works in most living rooms, patios, or garages (approximately 150-200 sq ft minimum)

Chefs typically arrive 30-45 minutes before your scheduled event start time for setup. This includes unpacking equipment, prepping ingredients, and testing the grill. Cooking begins right at your scheduled time!

The chef handles all cooking-related cleanup including the grill, utensils, food scraps, and work area. Guests' plates, cups, and general party cleanup are the host's responsibility. Most chefs are done packing within 20-30 minutes after the meal ends.

While extremely rare, if we must cancel (chef illness, emergency), we will:

  2. Offer a replacement chef of equal or higher quality
  3. If no replacement available, provide a full refund + $100 inconvenience credit
